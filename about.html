All About Me

Raised in the picturesque region of Bavaria, Germany, I had the privilege of growing up surrounded by the stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and mouthwatering cuisine that Bavaria is renowned for. From an early age, I developed a deep appreciation for the art of cooking. Upon arriving in the U.S., I quickly realized that the American culinary scene was a melting pot of flavors and traditions from all corners of the world. Intrigued by this multicultural culinary tapestry, I decided to follow my passion and dedicated the next 17 years of my life to the art of cooking. I honed my skills in various kitchens across the country, working in restaurants and catering companies, and embracing each opportunity to learn new techniques and expand my culinary repertoire. While my culinary career was fulfilling, I couldn't ignore the growing fascination I felt towards the digital realm and the boundless possibilities it offered. Intrigued by the ever-evolving world of technology and its potential for creative expression, I made a career shift and delved into the realm of web development. With relentless determination and an eagerness to learn, I immersed myself in coding languages, frameworks, and design principles. I embraced the challenges of mastering this new domain and discovered a newfound passion for creating innovative and intuitive digital experiences.
